RECYCLING

Who has to register as a recycler?

Any business that:

Recycles, reuses, and refurbishes one or all of the following electronic devices:

  1. Computers (such as desktops, all-in-one computers, laptops, notebooks, netbooks, and tablets)
  2. Computer monitors (any type, i.e., CRTs, flat panel displays, LCD displays, plasma displays)
  3. Printers (most types including multi functional machines – does not include floor-standing models)
  4. Televisions (any type, i.e.: CRTs, Flat panel displays, LCD displays, Plasma displays)
